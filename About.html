About Mica

Mica Diamond, MA, CPC

Mica Diamond is certified by iPEC (The Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching) which is a fully accredited program by the International Coach Federation (ICF). Mica is dedicated to partnering with individuals and groups  to create more balance, joy and fulfillment in their lives and uncover what truly motivates each individual.  Mica works with her clients as a team  to make these goals a reality and break through whatever has held them back in the past.  Mica believes that each person can live their life any way they choose, and the more we become aware of this, the more we are at the cause, instead of the effect of life.

Mica coaches individuals, families, groups and organizations as well as facilitating workshops. Topics include: finding balance in your life, career transition and relaunch, parenting, transition to the empty nest, stress reduction, managing your finances, communication skills, leadership development, and relationships.

Mica holds an M.A. in Organizational Psychology from Teacher's College, Columbia University and a B.A. in Business and Economics from Lafayette College. Mica operates her coaching practice in Westport, CT, Sag Harbor, NY, and NYC.
