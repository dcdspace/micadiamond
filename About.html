About Mica

Mica Diamond, MA, CPC

Mica Diamond is certified by iPEC (The Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching) which is a fully accredited program by the International Coach Federation (ICF).

Mica coaches individuals, families, groups and organizations as well as facilitating workshops and running coaching circles. Topics include: career transition, career relaunch, parenting, stress reduction, transition to the Empty Nest, relationships, work/life balance, and health & wellness.

Mica holds an M.A. in Organizational Psychology from Columbia University and a B.A. in Business and Economics from Lafayette College. Mica operates her practice from Westport CT, Sag Harbor NY, New York City and remotely via video conference/phone.
